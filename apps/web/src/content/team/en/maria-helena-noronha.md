Maria Helena Noronha is a retired university professor. She taught at California State University, Northridge, in Los Angeles, and at UNICAMP in Campinas, SP. She has always been deeply committed to social causes, especially those focused on social inclusion and education for children and adolescents in vulnerable situations. In Campinas, she collaborated with the Sociedade São Vicente de Paulo and currently works with Associação CrEsCER. In 2024, she joined ANSA with great enthusiasm, recognizing the significant impact of the organization on the lives of countless Brazilian women and children.
