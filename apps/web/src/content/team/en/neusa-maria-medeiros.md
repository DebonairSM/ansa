Neusa Maria Medeiros is ANSA's president and works passionately and spares no effort to help Brazil through ANSA, a dream made real. She prioritizes transparency, honesty and service. Born in Belém do Pará, the only child of cearense parents, she learned that life makes sense when knowledge is used for the welfare of others.
