The **Maria Mother of Life Association** is located in Quixadá, Ceará, and received a donation from ANSA of **R$ 8,858 (US$ 1,780)**.

The *Protecting Life Project* serves especially **pregnant teenagers** who are residents in at-risk areas or in the outskirts of Quixadá city.

The project aims to enable return to family, community reintegration, and above all, return to school. It develops activities for professional initiation, art-education, and preventive healthcare.

With the donation, the following were acquired: **10 tables, 70 chairs, 6 fans, 2 water fountains, and 1 overlock machine**.
