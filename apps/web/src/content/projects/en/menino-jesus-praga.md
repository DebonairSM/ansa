The **Menino Jesus de Praga Community Association** is located in Pacoti, Ceará, and received a donation from ANSA of **R$ 10,264 (US$ 2,000)**.

With ANSA's donation, an **industrial kitchen** was built for the community to work with fruits harvested from the region.

The *Cooking Does Good* project includes preparing jams and other products to be consumed and sold to raise funds for other community needs.

Approximately **300 people** will benefit from this kitchen project.
