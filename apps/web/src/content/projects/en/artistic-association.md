The **Artistic Educational Cultural Association** is located in Redenção, Ceará, and serves **140 students** aged 6 to 17 with the goal of promoting musical culture and bringing the community together.

With ANSA's donation of **R$ 12,000 (US$ 3,000)**, **three complete bathrooms** were built within the school premises.

Appliances for the common area were also purchased, including a stove, freezer and refrigerator, significantly improving the school's infrastructure and student comfort.

The *Redemption through music!* project demonstrates how culture and art can transform lives and communities.
