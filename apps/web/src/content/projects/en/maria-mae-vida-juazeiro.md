The **Maria Mother of Life Association** is located in Juazeiro do Norte, Ceará, and received a donation from ANSA of **R$ 11,100 (US$ 3,000)**.

The *Therapeutic Center Project* helps reintegrate at-risk youth into society. The Association shelters an average of **40 girls** in situations of social vulnerability and poverty, victims of chemical dependency.

With ANSA's donation, **30 beds** were acquired, significantly improving the center's infrastructure and the quality of life of the young women served.

This project demonstrates ANSA's commitment to the recovery and social reintegration of at-risk youth.
