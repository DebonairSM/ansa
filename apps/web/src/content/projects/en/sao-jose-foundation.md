The **São José Foundation** is located in Saboeiro, Ceará, and received a donation from ANSA of **R$ 9,537 (US$ 3,000)**.

With the donation, materials were purchased for the **construction of a new classroom**, which serves more than 40 children in two shifts.

It was also used to purchase **educational equipment**, which will enrich the daycare and tutoring program.

This project demonstrates ANSA's commitment to early childhood education and the development of underprivileged communities in Ceará.
